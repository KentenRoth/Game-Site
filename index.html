About the game

Dungeon Island is a small game that runs in the terminal. It is a text-based game, with different areas and enemies. You can move to new areas, attack enemies, drink health potions, and as you progress and take enemies out, you will level up. There are three different classes of characters that you can be, and each has its benefits and drawbacks. The Ninja has a lower HP, and does not block well, but has a higher dodge chance and potential to hit for double damage. The warrior is the middle ground with the chance to block and absorb damage, and It is also in the middle for the amount of HP that it has. The Viking has the most HP, and the best block ability, but a low dodge chance.

Why it was built

After several conversations with recruiters and people in the industry, I have seen a lot of feedback pointing towards me needing to learn Node and OOP. So I decided that one way I could tackle both of these things at the same time would be to build out a small game. This would allow me to learn how to learn OOP while building on my JS and learning the new things that Node brings to the table. I enjoyed the process of doing this. I ran into many obstacles, but the best part about coding is the feeling once you overcome the roadblocks in your code.
